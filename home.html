Welcome to Phi Gamma Delta's Michigan Chapter

This website serves to connect current brothers and alumni of Phi Gamma Delta "Fiji" at the University of Michigan. Contact information of alumni will be provided by members who created an account and have signed in. Furthermore, a blog will be updates weekly along with email announcements including any accomplishments from members of the frat. There will also be a section about our future events, some events including alumni like our beloved Pig Dinner. Lastly, there is an About page that will go over the frats history. Hopefully this serves as a platform for connections to be made.
