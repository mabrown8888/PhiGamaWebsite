About Phi Gamma Delta's Michigan Chapter

Our History

Phi Gamma Delta was founded in 1848 at Jefferson College in Canonsburg, Pennsylvania. Our chapter at the University of Michigan, Zeta Deuteron, was established in 1875 and has a long tradition of excellence in scholarship, leadership, and community service.

Our mission is to promote lifelong friendships, reaffirm high ethical standards and values, and foster personal development in the pursuit of excellence.

Our Philanthropy

Phi Gamma Delta at the University of Michigan is committed to giving back to our community. Our philanthropic efforts include supporting the C.S. Mott Children's Hospital, the American Cancer Society, and the UM Depression Center.

Each year, our chapter holds fundraisers and volunteer events to support these causes and make a positive impact in our community.

Joining Phi Gamma Delta

If you are interested in joining Phi Gamma Delta at the University of Michigan, please visit our contact page to get in touch with our recruitment team. We welcome men of all backgrounds who share our values of friendship, morality, and service.
